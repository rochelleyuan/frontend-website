About

My Story

Hi, I’m Rochelle, a UI/UX designer from California with an eye for detail and a passion for visual design. Oftentimes I look at something, anything, and think about how it could be improved to give people a better experience without even realizing it. I enjoy collaborating with others and bouncing back ideas and feedback. With my background working at a digital marketing agency, I’ve adapted to working with different industries in mind and always focused on the customer.

I’m currently available for full-time work in the Los Angeles County area or remotely. However, when I’m not hard at work, I like to bullet journal, make stickers/stationery, travel and have adventures with friends.
